¡Hello!

I’m Ivonne Tovar

UX/UI Designer

I’m a UX/UI designer with experience and passion for interface design, with an appreciation of aesthetics and originality, in favor of the development of innovative projects, with a critical capacity for analysis in visual communication, conceptualizing, crafting digital products and developing projects based on interpretation and analysis of the information.

Process is important to me. I want to make work with heart, purpose, and originality, with collaborators and customers who value the same things. I really enjoy solving problems simplifying the way users interact with products or services by combining information architecture, user research, usability, smart technology and a beautiful design, helping customers to expand their capacity for impact.
