Here is my story

I specialize in UI / UX design since 2011, where together with two friends we decided to create a start-up of mobile applications and video games development. At that time I had to learn to design for mobile devices from scratch, experimenting, sometimes doing and redoing everything again two or three times, because it was something very new and there were not many courses and tools to learn about this topic, we were kind of pioneers along with some other companies in my home country (Colombia) that also began to develop for mobile devices.

Shortly after creating the company, we moved to San Francisco California USA., since one of my partners wanted to study a Master in video game development in that city, so I ended up living there for four years; learning about the apps and videogame industry and also learning English. We had many mentors who taught us and guided us, I began to be more skilled, I learned how to manage time better and I began to update my knowledge with workshops and new courses that were coming out.

Finally, after 8 years of hard work the partnership with my partners fell apart, since then I find myself working independently, now I live in Chile and I work from home, looking for the opportunity to undertake a new challenge in my life, to share my experience and keep learning together with a new team.

¡Thank you for reading me!
